Homemade Pizza

This homemade pizza is crafted with a golden, crispy crust, layered with rich tomato sauce, melty cheese, and your favorite toppings. Simple to make and full of flavor, it’s the perfect way to bring the taste of a pizzeria right into your kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough

  • 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) instant yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

For the Toppings

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Tomato sauce (smooth or pureed)
  • Shredded Mozzerella Cheese
  • Basil leaves
  • Sliced cherry tomatoes

Step by Step

Making the Pizza Dough

  1. Place the warm water in the large bowl of a heavy duty stand mixer. Sprinkle the yeast over the warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until the yeast is dissolved.
  2. Add the flour, salt, sugar, and olive oil, and knead the dough with your hands. The dough should be a little sticky or tacky to the touch. If it's too wet, sprinkle in a little more flour.
  3. Spread a thin layer of olive oil over the inside of a large bowl. Place the pizza dough in the bowl and make sure its coated with the oil and cover the bowl with the dough with plastic wrap.
    • For a quick rise, place the dough in a warm place (75°F to 85°F) for 1 1/2 hours.
  4. Preheat the oven to 475°F for at least 30 minutes, preferably an hour.
  5. Remove the plastic cover from the dough. Dust your hands with flour and push the dough down so it deflates a bit, then divide the dough in half.Forming 2 round balls of dough, place each in its own bowl, cover with plastic, and let sit for 15 minutes.
  6. After 15 minutes, take one ball of dough and flatten it with your hands on a lightly floured work surface. Starting at the center and working outwards, use your fingertips to press the dough to 1/2-inch thick. Turn and stretch the dough until it will not stretch further.
    • If a hole appears in your dough, place the dough on a floured surface and push the dough back together to seal the hole.
  7. Brush the dough top with olive oil
  8. Repeat steps 6 and 7 with the second ball of dough.

Preparing the Toppings

  1. Wash basil leaves and slice cherry tomatoes in half. Note that you are not going to want to load up each pizza with a lot of toppings as the crust will end up not crisp that way.About a third a cup each of tomato sauce and cheese would be sufficient for one pizza

Baking the Pizza

  1. Spread dough top with tomato sauce and sprinkle with desired toppings
  2. Place pizza onto a baking pan or pizza stone and put into preheated oven.
  3. Bake pizzas one at a time, until the crust is browned and the cheese is golden for about 10 to 15 minutes. If you want, toward the end of the cooking time, you can sprinkle on a little more cheese.

Enjoy

Cut your pizza into slices and enjoy!
